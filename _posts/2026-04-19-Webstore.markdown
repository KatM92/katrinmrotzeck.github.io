  <b>🧉 Kombucharica Webstore & Scoby-gotchi PWA</b><br><br>
  <b>🌊 Overview</b> A Kombucha Webstore and "Scoby-gotchi" Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to bridge the gap between physical product consumption and digital brand loyalty. Built for the Caparica surf-brewery scene, it transforms the traditional beverage purchase into a digital ecosystem where your "Scoby" companion thrives based on real-world brewing habits.<br><br>
  <b>✨ Features</b><br>
  <b>Interactive Scoby-gotchi:</b> A minimalist digital pet that reacts to user interactions (taps and double-taps) and changes its state based on your "Fuel" levels.<br>
  <b>The "Fuel" Inventory:</b> A gamified tracking system for Tea, Sugar, and Fruit fuel.<br>
  <b>Automated Retention Loop:</b> Integrated with Zapier via Webhooks to trigger a "Hungry Scoby" nudge 10 days after a purchase.<br>
  <b>PWA Ready:</b> Fully optimized for mobile—can be installed as a standalone app on iOS and Android.<br>
  <b>Artisan Aesthetic:</b> A "vibe-coded" interface using a premium cream palette (#FFF1DB) and a clean, minimalist typography stack.<br><br>
  <b>⚙️ The Tech Stack</b><br>
  <b>Frontend:</b> React, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons, Framer Motion (for animations).<br>
  <b>Backend/Logic:</b> Webhooks & Zapier for automated customer lifecycle management.<br>
  <b>Persistence:</b> LocalStorage for zero-latency state management (offline-first).<br>
  <b>Hosting:</b> Lovable.<br><br>
  <b>🧠 Business Logic</b><br>
  <b>1. The Fuel Equation</b><br>
  The app translates product orders into digital rewards:<br>
  <b>Standard Bottle</b> = 1 Sugar Fuel + 1 Tea Fuel.<br>
  <b>Flavored Bottle</b> = +1 Fruit Fuel.<br><br>
  <b>2. The 10-Day Retention Nudge</b><br>
  The project features a built-in "decay" system. If 10 days pass without a "Fuel" update (tracked via last_purchase_date in the browser):<br>
  <b>The Scoby enters a sluggish "Hungry" state.</b><br>
  <b>This triggers a webhook to Zapier, which sends a personalized email nudge to the customer to re-engage with the brand and "refuel" their Scoby.</b><br><br>
  <b>🚀 Presentation for Founders</b><br>
  This prototype demonstrates a "Living Brand" concept. We have moved beyond simple loyalty cards to create a reason for the customer to open the app every day. It turns the product from a one-time purchase into an ongoing digital companionship.<br><br>
  Developed during an intensive 30-day "Vibe Coding" Caparica Founders Breakfast Unicorn Challenge.<br><br>
