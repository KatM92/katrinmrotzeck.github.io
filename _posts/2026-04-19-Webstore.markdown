"Kombucharica: The Scoby-gotchi PWA <br><br>
<b>🌊 Overview</b> Kombucharica is a "Scoby-gotchi" Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to bridge the gap between physical product consumption and digital brand loyalty. Built for the Caparica surf-brewery scene, it transforms the traditional beverage purchase into a digital ecosystem where your "Scoby" companion thrives based on real-world brewing habits.
<b> ✨ Features</b> 
<b> Interactive Scoby-gotchi:</b>  A minimalist digital pet that reacts to user interactions (taps and double-taps) and changes its state based on your "Fuel" levels.
<b>The "Fuel" Inventory:</b>  A gamified tracking system for Tea, Sugar, and Fruit fuel.
<b>Automated Retention Loop:</b>  Integrated with Zapier via Webhooks to trigger a "Hungry Scoby" nudge 10 days after a purchase.
<b>PWA Ready:</b>  Fully optimized for mobile—can be installed as a standalone app on iOS and Android.
<b>Artisan Aesthetic:</b>  A "vibe-coded" interface using a premium cream palette (`#FFF1DB`) and a clean, minimalist typography stack.

<b> ⚙️ The Tech Stack</b>
<b> Frontend:</b> React, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons, Framer Motion (for animations).
<b> Backend/Logic:</b> Webhooks & Zapier for automated customer lifecycle management.
<b> Persistence:</b> LocalStorage for zero-latency state management (offline-first).
<b> Hosting:</b> Lovable.

<b> 🧠 Business Logic</b>
<b> 1. The Fuel Equation</b>
The app translates product orders into digital rewards:
<b> Standard Bottle</b> = 1 Sugar Fuel + 1 Tea Fuel.
<b> Flavored Bottle</b> = +1 Fruit Fuel.

<b> 2. The 10-Day Retention Nudge</b>
The project features a built-in "decay" system. If 10 days pass without a "Fuel" update (tracked via `last_purchase_date` in the browser):
<b> The Scoby enters a sluggish "Hungry" state.</b>
<b> This triggers a webhook to Zapier, which sends a personalized email nudge to the customer to re-engage with the brand and "refuel" their Scoby.</b>

<b> 🚀 Presentation for Founders</b>
This prototype demonstrates a "Living Brand" concept. We have moved beyond simple loyalty cards to create a reason for the customer to open the app every day. It turns the product from a one-time purchase into an ongoing digital companionship.
