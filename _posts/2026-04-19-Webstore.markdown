# 🧉 Kombucharica: The Scoby-gotchi PWA
**Live Prototype:** https://coastal-brew-drop.lovable.app/

## 🌊 Overview
**Kombucharica** is a "Scoby-gotchi" Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to bridge the gap between physical product consumption and digital brand loyalty. Built for the Caparica surf-brewery scene, it transforms the traditional beverage purchase into a digital ecosystem where your "Scoby" companion thrives based on real-world brewing habits.

## ✨ Features
* **Interactive Scoby-gotchi:** A minimalist digital pet that reacts to user interactions (taps and double-taps) and changes its state based on your "Fuel" levels.
* **The "Fuel" Inventory:** A gamified tracking system for Tea, Sugar, and Fruit fuel.
* **Automated Retention Loop:** Integrated with Zapier via Webhooks to trigger a "Hungry Scoby" nudge 10 days after a purchase.
* **PWA Ready:** Fully optimized for mobile—can be installed as a standalone app on iOS and Android.
* **Artisan Aesthetic:** A "vibe-coded" interface using a premium cream palette (`#FFF1DB`) and a clean, minimalist typography stack.

## ⚙️ The Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** React, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons, Framer Motion (for animations).
* **Backend/Logic:** Webhooks & Zapier for automated customer lifecycle management.
* **Persistence:** LocalStorage for zero-latency state management (offline-first).
* **Hosting:** Lovable.

## 🧠 Business Logic
### 1. The Fuel Equation
The app translates product orders into digital rewards:
* **Standard Bottle** = 1 Sugar Fuel + 1 Tea Fuel.
* **Flavored Bottle** = +1 Fruit Fuel.

### 2. The 10-Day Retention Nudge
The project features a built-in "decay" system. If **10 days** pass without a "Fuel" update (tracked via `last_purchase_date` in the browser):
* The Scoby enters a sluggish **"Hungry"** state.
* This triggers a webhook to Zapier, which sends a personalized email nudge to the customer to re-engage with the brand and "refuel" their Scoby.

## 🚀 Presentation for Founders
This prototype demonstrates a **"Living Brand"** concept. We have moved beyond simple loyalty cards to create a reason for the customer to open the app every day. It turns the product from a one-time purchase into an ongoing digital companionship.
